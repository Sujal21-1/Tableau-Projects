## British Airways Dashboard

This dashboard analyzes British Airways customer review data to evaluate service quality 
across multiple dimensions, with a focus on cabin staff service. The analysis examines 
rating trends over time, differences by aircraft type, and geographic patterns in customer feedback.

### Key Features
- Average customer ratings across service categories
- Cabin staff service trends over time
- Service rating comparison by aircraft type
- Geographic distribution of customer reviews
- Interactive filters for traveler type, seat class, and aircraft

### Business Value
This dashboard helps airline stakeholders identify strengths and weaknesses in service delivery, 
understand how service perception varies by aircraft and region, and prioritize improvements 
that impact customer satisfaction.

### Skills Demonstrated
- KPI and trend analysis
- Customer experience analytics
- Interactive Tableau dashboard design
